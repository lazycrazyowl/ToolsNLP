iPhone 6: Most-wanted features

With the iPhone 5's first anniversary not far off, it's time to look ahead to the next iPhone. Here's a rundown of the features and design upgrades we'd most like to see.

by David Carnoy
June 19, 2013 7:05 AM PDT Updated: June 19, 2013 7:05 AM PDT
  • Follow @DavidCarnoy

NAK Studio's concept art for the iPhone 6 (click to enlarge).

(Credit: Antoine Brieux of NAK Studio)

The next iPhone -- or iPhones -- will most likely be released this fall, about a year after the iPhone 5 made its debut.

Will it be called the iPhone 6? Probably not. Rumor has it that Apple most likely will trot out the iPhone 5S, which will look cosmetically the same as the iPhone 5 but have some upgrades on the inside (see our regularly updated iPhone 5S rumor roundup page to get the latest dish).

But whatever it is called, I do have a wish list of what I'd like it to have.

Note: The 11 items in the list are ordered from least important to most in a reverse countdown. I've also included what I think are the chances of Apple actually implementing each feature.
